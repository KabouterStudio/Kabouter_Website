About studio Kabouter

Studio Kabouter is a solo game studio run by Laurens, focused on game design, game feel, physics, and crafting weirdly wonderful experiences for players. I have experience in gameplay programming, game design, 3D art, and prototyping, with a strong gameplay first mindset and a rapid prototyping approach to quickly turn ideas into playable experiences.
