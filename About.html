About

I'm a small, Belgium-based game developer on a big mission: to make games that are fun, full of heart, and bursting with culture and creativity. Whether you're a player or a partner, we’re here to bring joyful, meaningful experiences that resonate with players and inspire lasting connections with both our audience and business partners.

What do we make?

I won't just make what you ask. I'll sit down with you with a drink to figure it out together. That way, we can truly collaborate and deliver something even better.

Who am i?

I’m a game developer and designer focused on wacky and fun physics-based games and experiences.I specialize in game design, feel, and physics, ensuring that players have a weirdly wonderful experience while playing my games. My expertise spans gameplay programming, game design, 3D art, and prototyping. I maintain a gameplay-first approach, iterating through rapid prototyping to quickly bring ideas to life.
