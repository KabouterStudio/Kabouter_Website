Draai! (prototype)

Draai! is a physics-based puzzle adventure game where the player uses their spin powers to navigate levels and rescue their best friend, Rond.In this prototype, the player is tasked with bringing King Boulette back to his throne in a chaotic, minigolf-inspired level.

Idea & creation

Draai! is an idea that’s always been spinning around in the back of my mind, along with a bunch of other wacky concepts.It started as a physics-based brawler, then morphed into something more like a minigame collection à la WarioWare or Bishi Bashi.Now? It’s shaping up to be a single-player (and maybe co-op!) puzzle adventure game, somewhere between Katamari Damacy and Untitled Goose Game.
